Flow is a privacy-first YouTube and YouTube Music client. It provides a personalized feed using a completely local, on-device algorithm, giving you a modern discovery experience without tracking or accounts.

Features:

- Play videos and songs from YouTube and YouTube Music
- Local recommendation engine (FlowNeuro) that learns your preferences
- 100% on-device privacy with zero analytics or tracking
- No Google account required
- Background audio and Picture-in-Picture (PiP) video playback
- Built-in SponsorBlock to automatically skip sponsored segments
- Built-in DeArrow to replace clickbait titles and thumbnails
- Built-in Return YouTube Dislike (RYD)
- Download videos and audio for offline playback
- Dedicated music player with synchronized live lyrics
- Import subscriptions, playlists and history from YouTube, NewPipe and LibreTube
- Import Music library and playlists from YouTube Music and Metrolist
- Block specific channels and topics from appearing in your feed
- Automatically hide videos you have already watched or ignored
- Adjust audio pitch and playback speed
- Local playlist and queue management
- Swipe gesture controls for volume and brightness
- Modern Material 3 interface
- Export and backup your local algorithm data
